Position and Altitude Control of Quadrotor with Single Motor Failure

Addressed a high-complexity fault-tolerant control problem involving quadrotor flight under single motor failure conditions. Developed an implicit fault detection strategy without explicit failure signaling and implemented Incremental Nonlinear Dynamic Inversion (INDI) control to ensure stable attitude and altitude regulation.

The control architecture was validated through simulation-based studies focusing on robustness, fault recovery, and closed-loop performance under actuator loss scenarios.
